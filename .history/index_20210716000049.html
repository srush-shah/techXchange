techXchange was launched in 2018. It is a joint initiative of the FICCI UK and High Commission of India in UK, and supported by the various leading UK government agencies, UK industry associations. During the previous editions the program has been supported by the British High Commission in India, British Deputy High Commission Western India,  and several Partners viz. CIIE at IIMA, IAN (Indian Angel Network), Gujarat Government, iNDEXTb, to mention a few.

The programme is aimed at promoting and providing a global exposure to mature Startups from India and the UK to help them begin their journey of globalisation. It also aims to help Startup ecosystem stakeholders from India & the UK to interact and learn from each other.

This initiative is fully aligned with and supports the Start-up India program, which is a flagship initiative of the Government of India; intended to build a strong eco-system for nurturing innovation and Startups in the country. This is aimed at driving sustainable economic growth and generate large scale employment opportunities. The Government through this initiative aims to empower Startups to grow through innovation and design. To meet the objectives of this initiative, Government of India announced Startup India Action Plan that addresses all aspects of the Startup ecosystem.

With this background and to further accelerate the Indian Startup movement globally, techXchange is a platform that gives Indian Startups an exposure to expanding and collaborating abroad. It facilitates Indian Startups steer uncertainties, widen their perceptive of the start-up ecosystem around the world and help them accomplish their business on a global level, along with developing their transformative potential. Over the last two years, several Indian Startups have used this program to actually expand and engage with UK. 

In 2018 the programme had facilitated the launch of OYO in the UK. DPIIT, Government of India and Invest India used the techXchange event in Sept 2019 to launch their Online Portal along with DIT UK to collaborate, in the presence of the High Commissioner of India to UK, several UK Agencies like DIT (Department for International Trade), DCMS (Department for Digital, Culture, Media & Sports), London & Partners and Associations like techUK, Level 39, BDO, Manchester India Partnership, West Midlands Growth, Invest Wales, Scottish Development International, Ireland India Business Association and Partners like ICICI Bank - UK, Trails Trading, Indian Professionals Forum, IIMA Alumni London Limited, Instarem, Peach Ventures.

techXchange in a short time is now being well regarded as a serious effort to expand collaboration in the field of Startups from both sides- India & the UK.

The programe aims to facilitate:

  • Understanding about UK as market
  • Opportunity for partnership
  • Investment opportunities & Pitch
  • B2B meetings
  • Exchange of learnings & technology
  • Interaction with established Indian companies in UK

Sunil Parekh

India Co-Chair techXchange, Startup Ecosystem Evangelist & National Executive Committee Member, FICCI

He is India Chair of the techXchange initiative under the Indo UK Partnership Program for exchange of Start-ups. He is a Mentor to FICCI National Committee on StartUps and a founder member of the State Govt's Committee on StartUps in Gujarat. He is a Member of the Indian Angel Network (Asia's largest) & is actively involved with AI, IoT development. He is on the Board of several Incubators & Accelerators across India. He has actively supported DST GoI Program ‘Inspire' for the youth in India.

He is currently Senior Strategy Advisor two Indian MNCs - Zydus Cadila Healthcare Group, Jubilant Bhartia Group. He is also on the Board of Govt GNFC Ltd, GVFL Ltd.

He has 42 years' of working experience with corporates in India, Africa & the US.

He is an empanelled Arbitrator and a Fellow of the Indian Council of Arbitration ICA. He is Co-Chair Ficci in Gujarat and National Council Member of FICCI.

He is on the Board/Senate of several Universities incl IIT G'nagar, IIT RAM; Industry Bodies, AMA, Gujarat Chambers of Commerce & Industry.

He is the Founder Curator of the World Economic Forum Geneva's initiative - Global Shapers Initiative A'bad & a member of The Value Web Geneva. He is on the board of some leading NGOs.

He has received the following Awards:

  1. ET ICON Entrepreneur 2018 for Excellence in Corporate Advisory & Support to Start Ups
  2. National Awards incl Outstanding Contribution for National Development OCND at IIT Delhi
  3. Power 100 Eminent Personalities of Gujarat in 2011 & again in 2014

He continues to speak at several forums & national TV on current developments. He loves interacting with youth.

Param Shah

Director, FICCI - UK

Dr. Param Shah has been appointed as the Director of FICCI – UK since 2017. Param enjoys the distinction of steering trade bodies representing India's major business and industry entities at local, national and international levels, at a very young age. A PhD in International Marketing for SME and a Master's in Business Administration (MBA), Param has also studied law, commerce and intellectual property rights.

He specialises in International Marketing & Corporate Laws. He has over 17 years of experience ranging from academics to managing trade bodies. He is an alumnus of the prestigious International Visitor Leadership Programme (IVLP) of the U.S. Department of State. He was also associated with various Universities in India, as a Faculty in Finance and Corporate Laws. He has been academic advisor to the ICFAI University. He has more than 50 papers & articles on various aspects of Management and other economic issues to his credit.

Vipul Patel

Partner, Seed Investment Team, CIIE.CO

Vipul is a Partner in the Seed Investment team at CIIE.CO, the Innovation Continuum based at IIM Ahmedabad that spreads across incubation, acceleration, seed-funding, venture capital, and research. Since joining CIIE.CO in 2012, Vipul has worn many hats and contributed to multiple initiatives. He leads and manages CIIE.CO's portfolio of 160+ startups. He has conceptualised & managed multiple startup acceleration programs - both sector focussed and agnostic. He believes that delightful designs, emerging technologies, and Go-To-Market strategies drive organic growth in building great companies. He actively advises founders on how to get the formula right for orbit shift from Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to growth.

Vipul is a Chemical Engineer by training and started his career as an engineer with Hindalco Industries Limited. He holds an MBA in Entrepreneurship and had co-founded an e-commerce startup.

Sushmita Ghosh
